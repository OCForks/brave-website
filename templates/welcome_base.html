Brave puts a new spin on the infamous browser tab, helping hoarders and pruners alike.

Edit tab options

  • Tab Previews allow for quick glances and better hunting.

  • Tab Pages give you the big picture and prevent “micro-tab syndrome”. Even change how many are allowed per tab page.

  • Use Session Tabs to easily manage multiple social accounts.
